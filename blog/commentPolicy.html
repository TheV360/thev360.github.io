Comment Policy

This is just a few rules to ensure the comments don't become a dumpster fire.

  • Don't be a jerk.
  • Don't use slurs, and try to limit the amount of curses you use.
  • Try to be constructive.
  • Try to include at least three numbers or one symbol in your comment.
  • No spam.

If you violate any of these rules, I'll probably delete your comment.
